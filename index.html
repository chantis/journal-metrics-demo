The American Physical Society (APS) recognizes the limitations of using any single metric to evaluate the quality of research. In alignment with the principles of the Declaration on Research Assessment (DORA), APS emphasizes the importance of assessing research on its own merits rather than relying solely on journal-based metrics, such as the Impact Factor. We encourage the use of a variety of metrics to provide a more comprehensive understanding of the research's impact.
